FORTIS Lab

FORTIS stands for Foundations Of Robust Trustworthy Intelligent Systems. Derived from the Latin word "fortis," meaning strong or resilient, FORTIS Lab develops AI systems with a focus on robustness, trustworthiness, scalability, and open-source innovation. Addressing challenges such as out-of-distribution (OOD) detection, anomaly detection, and structured data analysis, the lab integrates graph learning and generative AI to solve real-world problems.
